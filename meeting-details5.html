March 27-29

Para sport

In addition to the high performance of disabled athletes, technology has a major role in the specific equipment such as wheelchairs and prostheses. During recent years, there has been a significant progress in the industry of the prosthesis and wheelchairs. The design of this equipment is specific to the biomechanical needs of athletes.

Most prostheses are increasingly strong and lightweight and adapt better to the movement of the athlete. Measurements are taken of the athletes prior to the creation of such equipment, as it should be comfortable for the athlete and allow him to achieve his goals, but unfortunately such equipment is overpriced, so the richer the athlete the more the more likely they are to succeed.
